Voting Precincts in East Baton Rouge Parish

There are three voting wards in East Baton Rouge Parish: Ward 1 (blue), Ward 2 (red), and Ward 3 (gray). Each ward is further divided into precincts. A precinct is defined in the Election Code as the smallest political unit of a ward having defined geographical boundaries. One polling place is required for each precinct. To learn more about voting in EBR Parish, consult the Voting Precinct and Polling Place Handbook. To register to vote, visit the Secretary of State's website.
